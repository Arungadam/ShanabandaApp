Shanabanda

Shanabanda is a village located in the Velgatur Subdivision of Jagitial district,Telangana, India.
Shanabanda has a population of 317 as recorded in the 2011 Census.
It falls under the jurisdiction of Shanabanda Gram Panchayat and the Velgatoor Block Panchayat in Velgatur District.
The village has 89 households and is identified by village code 571728.
Shanabanda is located in the 505526 postal area, contributing significantly to the rural administrative structure of Velgatur Sub-District.
